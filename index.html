Super Mario Collector

In order to save the Mushroom Kingdom, you must outwit Bowser at his numbers game! You will be assigned a random number at the start of the game. You must click on any of the four Mushroom Kingdom icons and discover thier point value. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each icon is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the icon values.
